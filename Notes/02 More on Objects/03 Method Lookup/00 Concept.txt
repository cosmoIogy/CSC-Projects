Polymorphism:
    -poly - multiple
    -morph - form
    -ism - a practice or philosophy
    The idea of having different ways to interface with something
    
Method lookup:
    A form of Polymorphism
    Used to find the right method to call in a class heirarchy
    Ex : if a subclass doesnt have the __str__ method but its superclass does, them the method in the superclass will be called
