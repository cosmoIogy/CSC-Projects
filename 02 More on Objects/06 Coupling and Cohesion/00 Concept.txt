Unit: any reasonably self-contained compontent
    Ex : a function, a class, a file, a folder, etc...

Coupling:
    The linking of two seperate units
    Goals is to have loosely coupled units
    Tightly coupled units are generally considered bad practice

Cohesion:
    refers to the number of and diversity of tasks that a single unit is responsible for 
    High Cohesion is good, because it means it is responsible for one single task
    Low Cohesion means a unit is responsible for lots of things
